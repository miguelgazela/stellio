Our projects

Bulla

The Bulla authenticity service is an easy way to verify if you are buying an authentic product. The service brings added value to the customer and to the brand itself, while helping fighting counterfeiting.

The Bulla system is an authentication service used to validate the authenticity of luxury products. The technology behind the system is called NFC, and it is available in most Android smartphones sold nowadays.

The system includes two components: the Bulla Android application, that is meant to be used by customers to authenticate the products; and the Bulla web application that is used by the brands to manage their products. Despite being two different applications, they work by accessing the same RESTful API, which provides all the features and information they require.

Health +

Health+ is a project that brings together students of computer Engineering master with multimedia students and is consists on the sale of several coupons related to consultations and medical examination service.

Health+ is a system that will bring together three components: a website for hospitals where the institutions can insert the coupons, a website that allows the customer to buy those coupons and take advantage of the promotions and a mobile application for Android devices, which will allow the customer to have in his phone the same features as the website.

So with Health+, our goal is to increase the quality of people's life with the disclosure of consultation / medical exams at a reduced price.

Museu Soares dos Reis

Soares dos Reis National Museum is housed in the Palace of Gargoyles, in Porto in Miragaia, being a building from the eighteenth century that was adapted to perform this function. It is the oldest public museum in the country that has a history of more than two centuries. The palace was the residence of several famous figures during the historical events such as the Napoleonic wars and the Siege of Porto.

The museum has undergone renovations from 1992 to 2001, allowing the existance of spaces for permanent exhibition of painting and sculpture and decorative arts.

XRQuest

XRQUEST is one of the Projects currently being developed by Stellio, whose client is Exerlife Unipessoal Lda.

The required project is the development of an application to monitor the physical activity of children. This application must be developed for Android OS and have a simple interface. The main features of the application are a set of scheduled tests to monitor the child’s physical condition. The application will also be able track physical activity during the day.

The objective of the application is to use the information gathered and display it to the user, and if thats the case send notifications when the amount of physical activity is below a set threshold.
